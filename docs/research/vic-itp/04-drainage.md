### Test Methods & Frequencies Summary — Section 701

| Test | Standard / Method | Frequency | Acceptance Value |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bedding/backfill grading | AS 1289 / RC 316.00 | 1 per 1,000 tonnes (Table 701.231) | Per Table 701.041/042 |
| Plasticity Index | AS 1289 | 1 per 1,000 tonnes (Table 701.231) | PI max 20% |

| Pipe dimensional accuracy | AS 4058 | Per delivery | Min 95% of nominal diameter |

| CCTV inspection | WSA 05:2020 | Every pipe run, after earthworks to subgrade | Structural grade 1, Service grade 1 |
